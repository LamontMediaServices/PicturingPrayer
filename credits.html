Support for this project was originally provided by the University CIO's office and the Library Instructional Technology Fellows (LITF) program. LITFs are Harvard students that work with librarians to create and integrate educational digital content into the online academic environment. In 2016 the project was revisited and migrated to a new hosting provider.
